Social Responsibility

Farmland Rainwater Harvesting Systems has setup its Research and Manufacturing Plant in an area of 5 acres At Hadihalli Village in Malalur Gram Panchayath limits of Chikmagalur District- known for its Coffee Fragrance. Using advanced and modern technology, the plant had been conserving rain water drop by drop from the last three years and has played a pivotal role in improving the groundwater source. Our Company has never forgotten its social responsibility at any point of time. Whenever any calamities occurred in the country the Company feels proud to say that it has fulfilled its responsibility with great interest. It is known that COVID-19 pandemic wreaked havoc in 2020 and affected all sectors. During this period Farmland Rainwater Harvesting Systems, realizing its responsibility, responded positively to problems faced by the people.
