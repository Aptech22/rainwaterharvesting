Rain Water Harvesting for commercial

Commercial Rainwater Harvesting

Commercial rainwater harvesting is the collection, storage and filtration of reclaimed rainwater from the roof area of commercial buildings. Reused rainwater can provide a sustainable water supply for toilet flushing, irrigation and vehicle washing plants.

Rainwater Harvesting can help your commercial building to reap the benefits of water conservation. Reducing reliance on mains water supplies automatically gives your facility access to water savings, reduced carbon emissions, and significant savings on monthly water bills.

As UK market leaders for rainwater harvesting, we have a range of water reuse solutions available for buildings of all shapes, sizes and uses. Our team of experts have years of experience providing bespoke solutions to many major UK buildings. See our range below.

Non-Presseurid System

A non pressurised system will gravity feed the water to its point of use from a header tank. A storage tank (either above or below ground) with a control unit is also supplied to complete the system

Presseurised System

A pressurised system is similar to a non pressurised system; with the exception that water enters the control system and flows via a pressurisation unit without the need for a header tank.

Active Attenuation

The Stormsaver Active Attenuation brings together Attenuation and Rainwater Harvesting thanks to Stormharvester weather prediction technology. The system counts towards 100% of attenuation volumes on-site at the same time as harvesting and re-using rainwater for a range of applications.

Combi System

Our unique Combi rainwater harvesting system, is our best seller, without a doubt! Rainwater is stored awaiting distribution by the energy efficient variable speed booster set; providing water at pressure to a range of applications without the need for large header tanks, but allowing the mains water top up above ground and protecting the life of the submersible pumps.
